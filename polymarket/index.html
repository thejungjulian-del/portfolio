⚡ Headline insight
Profitable traders win on execution, not prediction — the one replicable, no-speed-race edge is slow news-driven dip-reversion.
Buy the crowd's overreaction dips in liquid geopolitics/politics markets and flip ~12h later, before the bounce fades. The edge is monotonic in dip depth: shallow dips (<7¢) are dead after the ~1¢ spread, but deep dips bounce hard — so capital is concentrated on depth via conviction sizing (1–5 units). Crypto and sports show no dip edge and are excluded.
