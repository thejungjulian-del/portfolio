Research data-collector, not a trader. This service snapshots public YES/NO prices and order books from Hyperliquid's HIP-4 on-chain prediction markets (fully-collateralized contracts that settle $0 / $1) into a local SQLite database, to characterize what moves these markets. It places no orders and needs no wallet — all reads hit the public /info endpoint.
